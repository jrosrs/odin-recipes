Batata recheada

Description

Batata recheada is a common dellish from the brazilian cousine, that you can specially find on birthday parties.

It consists of dough that is fried and filled with content of your preference, such as chocolate or cheese.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Cheese
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil
  2. Prepare potato
  3. Put potato in oil
  4. Eat!
